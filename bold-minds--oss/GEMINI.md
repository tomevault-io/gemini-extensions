# CODE_OF_CONDUCT Standard for Bold Minds

## Purpose
Every Bold Minds repository MUST have a CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md file that establishes community standards and expected behavior.

## Required Standard
- MUST use **Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct version 2.1**
- Ensures consistency across all Bold Minds projects
- Widely recognized and adopted in open source community

## File Requirements

### File Name
- Must be named exactly `CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md`
- Placed in the root directory of the repository

### Required Sections

#### 1. Title
- "Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct" as H1 heading

#### 2. Our Pledge Section
Must include pledge statement covering:
- Harassment-free experience for everyone
- Regardless of:
  - Age, body size
  - Visible or invisible disability
  - Ethnicity, sex characteristics
  - Gender identity and expression
  - Level of experience, education
  - Socio-economic status
  - Nationality, personal appearance
  - Race, caste, color
  - Religion
  - Sexual identity and orientation
- Commitment to open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community

#### 3. Our Standards Section
Two subsections required:

**Examples of Positive Behavior:**
- Demonstrating empathy and kindness
- Being respectful of differing opinions and experiences
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback
- Accepting responsibility and apologizing
- Focusing on community benefit

**Examples of Unacceptable Behavior:**
- Sexualized language or imagery
- Sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting comments, personal attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing private information without permission
- Other inappropriate conduct

#### 4. Enforcement Responsibilities Section
Must specify:
- Community leaders' role in clarifying and enforcing standards
- Right to take corrective action
- Right to remove, edit, or reject contributions
- Communication of moderation decisions

#### 5. Scope Section
Must define where code applies:
- All community spaces
- When representing community in public spaces
- Examples of representation

#### 6. Enforcement Section
Must specify:
- How to report issues
- Reporting channels (GitHub issues or direct contact with maintainers)
- Promise of prompt and fair investigation
- Privacy and security of reporter

#### 7. Attribution Section
Must include:
- Reference to Contributor Covenant
- Version number (2.1)
- Link to https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct.html
- Homepage link: https://www.contributor-covenant.org

## Template Compliance
- MUST use exact text from `https://github.com/bold-minds/oss/blob/main/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md`
- Do NOT modify the standard Contributor Covenant text
- Only customization allowed is in Enforcement section for contact details

## Enforcement Customization
The Enforcement section can specify:
- Preferred reporting method (GitHub issues and/or direct contact)
- Project maintainer contact information
- Bold Minds may update this to specify: security@boldminds.tech or specific maintainer contacts
