# SoulSpeak

> Tome by [chengzi0103](https://github.com/chengzi0103/SoulSpeak) — distributed by [TomeVault](https://tomevault.io)

**SoulSpeak** is an emotionally driven AI companion framework that brings the voice of loved ones back into your life. By combining custom speech synthesis (TTS), large language models (LLMs), and memory-based dialogue systems, SoulSpeak offers a deeply personal, soothing voice experience — in Chinese and English.
