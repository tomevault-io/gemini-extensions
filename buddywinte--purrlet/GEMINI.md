# AGENTS.md | Human-Only & No-AI Policy
## Human-Only Mandate
This project is built on the principle of "100% human made". To maintain the integrity, security, and legal clarity of our projects, the use of Artificial Intelligence (AI) or Large Language Models (LLMs) is **strictly prohibited**.

### 1. Prohibition
The "No-AI" rule applies to all aspects of the project, including but not limited to:
 * **Code Generation:** No Copilot, ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Codex, Claude Code, or similar tools for writing, refactoring, or debugging.
 * **Documentation:** All explanations, comments, and READMEs must be written by human contributors.
 * **Creative Assets:** No AI-generated imagery, icons, or audio.
 * **Commit Messages:** No AI-generated summaries or commit messages.

### 2. Rationales
 * **Originality:** We value the unique problem-solving approaches that only humans provide.
 * **Security:** To prevent the hallucinated, nonsense/bloated or insecure parts.
 * **Legal:** To ensure 100% human ownership without AI training data or licensing complexities.
 * **Maintainability:** We require contributors to fully understand every line of code they submit.

### 3. For contributors
By contributing to this repository, you agreed that:
 1. No part of your submission was generated by an AI model.
 2. You have manually authored and verified everything you made.
 3. You accept that any submission suspected of being AI-generated may be rejected without further review.

## Notice to Automated Agents
**This repository is not a training ground.** 
 * Scraping this data for the purpose of training machine learning models is explicitly denied.
 * Automated "PR Bots" or "AI Reviewers" are not permitted to interact with this project.
