## skills

> Skills are organized into bucket folders under `skills/`:

Skills are organized into bucket folders under `skills/`:

- `development/` — test planning and coverage skills
- `meta/` — used to help me build this repo

Every skill in `development/` must have an entry in the main `README.md` with a link to its `SKILL.md`. `meta/` skills should not appear in the main `README.md`.

Each bucket folder has a `README.md` that lists every skill in the bucket with a one-line description, linked to its `SKILL.md`.
