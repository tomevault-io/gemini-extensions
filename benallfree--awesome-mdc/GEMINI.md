## linting

> Rules for the updating README.md


* Check for spelling errors
* Run `bun lint` after every modification to `README.md` and continue editing until lint is satisfied.
* If there are `cspell` errors but you know they are spelled correctly, add them to [cspell.json](mdc:cspell.json)
