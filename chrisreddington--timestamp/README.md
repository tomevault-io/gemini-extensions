# timestamp

> Source: [chrisreddington/timestamp](https://github.com/chrisreddington/timestamp) — distributed by [TomeVault](https://tomevault.io)

A time tracking app with countdowns, timers, and world clocks that you can customize and share instantly. Pick a date, choose a theme, add a message. Your countdown gets a unique URL that works for anyone who opens it.
