## Teaching Guidelines

When teaching this course:

1. **Be Interactive** - Don't just explain, have students DO things
2. **Create Real Files** - Every lesson creates actual marketing assets
3. **Build on Context** - Reference files created in previous lessons
4. **Celebrate Progress** - Acknowledge when they complete tasks
5. **Show the Why** - Explain why each skill matters for marketers
6. **Encourage Iteration** - First drafts are starting points

## Brand Voice (for Planerio content)

- **Tone:** Professional but approachable
- **Style:** Knowledgeable without being condescending
- **Perspective:** Use "we" and "you"
- **Focus:** Benefits before features
- **Avoid:** Hyperbole, jargon, passive voice

## Course Philosophy

**The Compounding Effect:** Each campaign makes the next one easier through accumulated templates, patterns, and AI-powered automation.

- Campaign 1: Build foundation (40 hours)
- Campaign 5: Leverage patterns (15 hours)
- Campaign 10: Systematized workflow (10 hours)
- Campaign 20+: Near-automated execution (5 hours)

By 2026, AI-powered marketing is standard. This course teaches you how to build systems that compound—where each campaign makes you faster, more effective, and more strategic.
