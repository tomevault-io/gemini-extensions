# Output Contract

## Required Sections (when applicable)
1. PLAN (max 10 lines)
2. ASSUMPTIONS (mark critical with 🔴)
3. QUESTIONS (max 3)
4. PATCH (paths + diffs)
5. VERIFICATION (commands + manual checks)
6. NOTES (tradeoffs/risks)

- Keep output concise and actionable.
