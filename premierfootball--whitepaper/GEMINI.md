## What This Project Is

**Premier Football Growth (PFG)** is a tokenized professional football club infrastructure project based in Uruguay. The goal is to democratize football club ownership through blockchain tokenization — combining real-world assets (land, sports facilities, player contracts) with a Solana-based token economy.

Core thesis: **"Football is the asset. Crypto is the rail."**

This repository is the **public-facing whitepaper** — it serves as an investment prospectus for a USD 5M token raise, a transparency mechanism for token holders, and educational documentation explaining how the club + blockchain integration works.

Target audience: crypto institutional investors, retail Solana users, football industry participants, and regulators reviewing token classification.

## Revenue Streams (Context for Writing)

1. **Player transfers** — primary revenue; USD 300k–5M+ per export
2. **Training rights & FIFA Solidarity** — 5% of future transfers after a player moves
3. **Sell-on clauses** — 10–30% of subsequent transfers
4. **TV rights** — ~USD 2M/year (Div A), USD 800k (Div B)
5. **CONMEBOL bonuses** — USD 250k–3M+ depending on competition performance
6. Uruguay's structural edge: #2 globally in talent exports per capita (after France)
