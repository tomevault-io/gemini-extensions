## promptminder

> Solo prompt power users managing and refining prompt libraries as part of their daily AI workflow. They need fast, reliable control over creating, organizing, versioning, and testing prompts without visual noise or consumer-style distraction.

## Design Context

### Users
Solo prompt power users managing and refining prompt libraries as part of their daily AI workflow. They need fast, reliable control over creating, organizing, versioning, and testing prompts without visual noise or consumer-style distraction.

### Brand Personality
Modern, minimal, black-and-white. The product should feel confident, precise, and professional. The interface should reinforce trust, competence, and control rather than playfulness or novelty.

### Aesthetic Direction
Stay close to the current product direction, with Vercel as the clearest visual reference for overall restraint and polish. Prefer a light-mode-first experience with a monochrome foundation, using black, white, and slate neutrals as the primary palette. Accent color can be used sparingly for interaction states, hierarchy, and status, but the overall feel should remain restrained and minimal. Avoid generic "AI-style" visuals such as neon gradients, glowing effects, sci-fi motifs, or overly futuristic marketing aesthetics.

### Design Principles
1. Design for confidence first: every screen should feel reliable, clear, and deliberate.
2. Keep the visual language minimal: remove decorative excess and favor disciplined spacing, typography, and hierarchy.
3. Use monochrome as the default: black, white, and neutral grays should carry most of the interface, with accents used intentionally rather than continuously.
4. Prioritize workflow clarity over spectacle: optimize for fast scanning, efficient actions, and strong content readability.
5. Keep motion subtle and functional: animation should support orientation and feedback, not call attention to itself.
