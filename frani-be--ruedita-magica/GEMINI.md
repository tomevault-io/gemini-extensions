# Design System (Windsurf AI Brand Identity)
- **Theme:** Strict Dark Mode. Backgrounds must be deep black or very dark grey (e.g., `bg-slate-950`).
- **Color Palette:** Use electric accents for contrast against the dark background.
    - Primary Accents: Neon Blue, Cyan, Electric Purple.
    - Text: White or light gray for readability.
- **Wheel Aesthetics:** The wheel shouldn't look like a carnival game. It should look like a futuristic UI component. Use gradients for segments and glowing borders.
- **Typography:** Clean, modern sans-serif (Inter, Roboto Mono, or similar tech fonts).
