# designStack — Design Bible Context

**Read `design/DESIGN-BIBLE.md` before making any changes to files matched by this rule.**

The Design Bible contains:
- Your exact brand colors (hex codes)
- Your font families and type scale
- Your spacing system (base unit and scale)
- Your border radius rules
- How your buttons, cards, inputs, and navigation should look
- The feeling and audience for this product

**Apply every rule from the Design Bible when:**
- Writing or editing CSS / Tailwind classes
- Creating or modifying components
- Choosing colors, font sizes, or spacing values
- Reviewing whether a design looks right

**If a change you're making conflicts with the Design Bible**, say so before making it:
> "The Design Bible says primary buttons should use `[value]`, but you're asking me to use `[other value]`. Want me to update the Bible, or follow the existing rule here?"

**If you add a new component or pattern not yet in the Design Bible**, suggest adding it:
> "This is a new pattern — want me to add the rules for it to your Design Bible so it stays consistent?"
